<h1 align="center">AgroPulse: Smart Vegetable Price Viewer 🥦</h1>

## 📋 Overview
**AgroPulse** is a project aimed at developing a smart, accurate, and daily updated wholesale vegetable price viewer. 

**Group:** 14  
**Intake:** 42  
**Department:** BioSystems Technology  
**Faculty:** Faculty of Technology  
**University:** General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University (KDU)  

## 📖 Introduction
In many rural and urban areas in Sri Lanka, farmers and customers face difficulty accessing reliable and updated vegetable prices. They have to rely on physically visiting economic centers or directly contact shops to find out daily vegetable prices. This often leads to unfair pricing and market inefficiencies due to miscommunication and basic human errors. As a solution for this, we propose a web-based platform that provides a daily updated digital price list of vegetables.

## 🎯 Purpose & Goals
- **Purpose:** To outline the development of a user-friendly web-based platform that displays daily updated wholesale vegetable prices to farmers and customers. By offering real-time price data, the platform will empower farmers to analyze market trends, optimize their harvest schedules, and make smarter sales decisions.
- **Project Aim:** To develop a smart, accurate, and daily updated wholesale vegetable price viewer.

### Project Objectives
**General Objective:**
- To design a responsive website for viewing daily vegetable prices.

**Specific Objectives:**
- To create an admin panel to update prices easily.
- To provide multi-language support (Sinhala, Tamil, English).
- To ensure mobile accessibility for rural users.
- To help farmers make informed pricing decisions.

## 📋 Project Scope
This project will focus on developing a web application only. It will not include mobile apps or payment features. The site will display prices and offer basic data management tools.
